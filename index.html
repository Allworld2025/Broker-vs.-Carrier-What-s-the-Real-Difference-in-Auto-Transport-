Understanding the Basics

When it comes to shipping your car, the terms broker and carrier often create confusion. Let's break them down and see how a trusted company like AmeriFreight fits in.

Who is a Carrier?

A carrier is the company that physically transports your vehicle from point A to point B. These are the ones behind the wheel—handling the actual logistics of loading, moving, and delivering your car.

What Does a Broker Do?

A broker, on the other hand, acts as a matchmaker between customers and carriers. They coordinate transport, manage paperwork, and ensure your vehicle is matched with the best-rated, available transporter.
